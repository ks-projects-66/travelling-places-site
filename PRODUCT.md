# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Discerning Australian and international travellers who value personal guidance, experienced judgement, and carefully managed details over self-service booking. Many are planning cruises, complex itineraries, milestone journeys, or premium travel and want a trusted advisor who knows them.

## Product Purpose

Present Travelling Places as Tamborine Mountain's established, personal travel company and make it easy for prospective clients to understand the team's expertise, recognise the value of its Virtuoso affiliation, and begin a conversation about a journey.

## Brand Personality

Personal, assured, and well-travelled. The voice should feel like an experienced advisor welcoming a client into a calm local office: warm without being casual, refined without being aloof, and confident without exaggeration.

## Anti-references

Avoid the crowded booking-engine look, generic tropical-stock-photo travel sites, excessive luxury cues, beige editorial styling, glossy corporate jargon, and interfaces that make visitors compare dozens of products before speaking to a person.

## Design Principles

- Lead with people and trusted relationships.
- Make expertise easy to understand, not loud.
- Keep the path from inspiration to conversation short.
- Balance global access with Tamborine Mountain warmth.
- Let careful details demonstrate the service promise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA with strong contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-operable navigation and dialog controls, descriptive image text, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts from small phones through large desktop screens.
